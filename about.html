About & Contact

Tango Boise Inc. is a community-driven nonprofit bringing Argentine tango to the Treasure Valley.

Our Mission

Tango Boise Inc. exists to nurture and grow the Argentine tango community in Boise, Idaho. We organize classes, practicas, milongas, and annual festivals that bring dancers together — from curious beginners taking their first steps to experienced dancers seeking connection.

As a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit, we are sustained by the energy and generosity of our community. Everything we do — every class, every event, every milonga — is in service of the dance and the people who love it.

Tango Boise has been serving the Treasure Valley since 2014.
