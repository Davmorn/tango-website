Our Commitment

Health, Safety & Conduct

Tango Boise is committed to providing a healthy, safe, inclusive, and comfortable dance experience for everyone — without discrimination of any kind.

Health & Illness

To keep our community healthy, please do not attend Tango Boise events if any of the following apply to you:

  • You have tested positive for COVID-19 within the past 10 days
  • You have been exposed to COVID-19 in the last 5 days
  • You are unwell with symptoms including fever, cough, congestion, sore throat, headache, body aches, nausea, vomiting, diarrhea, or difficulty breathing

Tango Boise reserves the right to institute additional health and safety protocols as circumstances require, including requirements for proof of vaccination, negative test results, or masks.

Safety Reporting

If you experience or witness any of the following, please report it immediately to an organizer, instructor, DJ, or staff member:

  • Injuries or medical emergencies
  • Safety concerns or hazards
  • Threats, harassment, or inappropriate behavior

We take all reports seriously and will act promptly to address any situation.

Harassment Policy

Harassment in any form will not be tolerated. This includes, but is not limited to:

  • Verbal abuse, intimidation, or stalking
  • Unwanted physical contact
  • Sexual harassment of any kind

Anyone engaging in harassment will be asked to leave immediately and may be banned from future events. If you feel unsafe, please find an organizer or staff member right away.

Dance Invitations & Cabeceo

Dance invitations may be extended via cabeceo (eye contact and a nod) or a polite verbal request. Declining an invitation requires no explanation and must be respected without question, comment, or pressure. This applies to all participants regardless of gender or experience level.

Physical Contact & Embrace

Partners should maintain an embrace style and physical distance that is comfortable for both people. Any leg contact or closer embrace style used in contemporary tango requires explicit, verbal consent from both partners before being attempted.

If a partner signals discomfort — verbally or non-verbally — adjust immediately and without argument.

Photography & Video

Tango Boise Inc. may photograph or record attendees at events for organizational business purposes such as social media, marketing, and archival. By attending, you acknowledge this possibility.

Attendees who wish to film or photograph others should seek consent before recording. Tango Boise is not responsible for non-consensual recordings made by other attendees.

Questions or Concerns?

If you have questions about these policies or need to report an incident, please reach out to us directly.
