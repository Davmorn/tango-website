Support Your Community

Membership

Your membership supports Argentine tango in Boise and comes with real perks — discounted access to events and exclusive member announcements.

Suggested Contribution

$25–$50
Minimum $25 for membership benefits. All membership fees are tax-deductible donations to Tango Boise Inc., a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit.

Member Benefits

  • Preferred access and/or discounted pricing to Tango Boise events
  • Exclusive member email list with special event discounts
  • Discount codes for tango weekends and festivals
  • Automated email receipt for tax records
  • Satisfaction of supporting live tango culture in Boise

Tax Deductibility

As a 501(c)(3) nonprofit, Tango Boise membership fees qualify as tax-deductible charitable donations. Certain benefits (event discounts, preferred access, token items) are disregarded for tax purposes. You'll receive an automated receipt by email.

Membership Issues?

If you're having trouble with your membership status or communications, contact us and we'll get it sorted out.

Ready to Join?

Membership is processed securely through MoonClerk. Choose your contribution level at checkout.
